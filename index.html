Things to do in Madison

While you are here, enjoy yourself like a local Madisonian. Here is a list of the stuff that we love to do

  • Grab a beer or several at Coopers
  • Eat some cheese curds and sip and old fashioned at The Old Fashioned
  • Explore Epic (if you have time)
  • Grab a Guinness at Brochah
  • Play some pool at the Great Dane
  • If you want a Wisconsin farm-2-table meal check out Graze
  • Get a late night steak sandwich at Tornado (after 10pm)
  • Check out the ever rotating $4 beer selection at Side Door
